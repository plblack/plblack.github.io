Describe the gameplay. 2 player hit a ball back and forth horizontally until one player misses the ball. Everytime the player misses the ball the other player recives a point, there is usually a score limit.
What are the conditions when the game begins? Both players start with 0 points.
Does the game have an end? If so, what are the conditions for when it ends? Yes, it has an end when one player reaches a certain score.
What are the visual game components? For example, in Bouncing Box, the game components were the board and the box. A ball, two vertical lines(the players), and the board.
Which will be static? The board
Which will be animated? The ball and the players(vertical lines) will be animated.
What data will you need to manage each game component? SpeedX, speedY, positionX, positionY, and points. 
What events will occur in this game? (timer events, keyboard events, clicking events?) Keyboard events and point events
How do those events affect the data of the program? Whenever the ball goes past the player the other player will gain a point. The position of the ball is reset.
